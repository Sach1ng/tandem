## How to sound like yourself (read this once, then forget it)

- **Talk like you're explaining it to a friend at lunch.** If a line feels like marketing copy in your mouth, change the words on the spot — the timing cues matter, the exact words don't.
- Use contractions. Small imperfections ("so, yeah—", a restart) sound human. Don't re-record for them.
- Every screen now has more time than the words need. **If you finish early, stay silent** — the visuals keep moving and you sound confident.
- `[pause]` = short beat (~1s). `[hold]` = longer, let the screen do the work (~2s). **Bold** = the word to lean on.
